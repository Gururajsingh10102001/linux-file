The college was founded as Government Engineering College, Ujjain, in 1966 by the government of Madhya
Pradesh, with a view to improve technical education. The college was declared autonomous by the state
government in 1997. Under this arrangement, the college is governed by a governing body, whose chairman
is the minister of the Man Power Planning Department of the state government and the secretary is the
college's principal. The college is recognized by the All India Council of Technical Education.
